Crab Mac&Cheese

Wanting to add some delicacy on your Mac and Cheese? This Crab Mac&Cheese is perfect for you, adding the lump of crab meat with white cheddar cheese, and creamy Boursin, seems hard but it's really quick and easy.

  • 1/2 pound of penne pasta
  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons of all purpose flour
  • 4 cups of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of any seafood seasoning
  • 3 cup of shredded sharp white cheddar cheese
  • 5.2 ounce of Garlic & Herbs Soft Spreadable Cheese (Boursin) in chunks.
  • salt
  • Hot sauce
  • 2 ounce of lump crab meat

Directions

  1. In a large pot, boil a lightly salted water. Cook the pasta, stir occasionally for 12 minutes until the pasta is tender but also firm. Drain and let it aside keeping it warm.
  2. In a pan preferably a saucepan, add the butter with the flour but whisking it under medium heat. Slowly pour the milk while whisking, and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes. When it comes to a boil turn off the heat, add the seafood seasoning and the cheeses and whisk until the cheese is melted and becomes smooth.
  3. Add the pasta and slowly add the crab meat. Add the hot sauce and stir slowly.
