Banana Chocolate Chip Bread

Whenever you have an unexpected visits or want a quick way to show you cooking skills, but don't have anything prepare and don't have time to run the store.

This quick and easy Banana Chocolate Chip Bread are perfect for breakfast, snack, or even dessert. They last for about four days if store correctly, in between an airtight container/zip-tog bag works fine with a paper towel at the bottom and on top of the bread. You can even freeze the whole bread or by slices if you don't want to thaw the bread by wrapping it in one layer of storage wrap with one layer of aluminum foil.

Ingredients needed for the Banana Bread Recipes

  • 2 Cups of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 teaspoon of Leaveners: Anything that help make the bread rise such as Baking soda and Baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt: Obviously not too much salt that could make the bread salty, but very light salt to enhance the overall flavor.
  • Mash Bananas: Three very ripe bananas, the more spots, the better
  • 1 tablespoon of Milk: Needed to add moisture
  • 1 teaspoon of Cinnamon: Use to add warmth
  • 1 cup of Sugar; Preferably white sugar but can also use a mixture of brown and white sugars.
    • 1/2 cup of soften butter: Help with keeping the bread from sticking with the pan
  • 2 large Eggs: Help keep the batter together and adds moistures
  • 1 cup of Chocolate Chips: What's a Banana Chocolate Chip Bread without the Chocolate Chip.

Steps

There will be a step-by-step recipe underneath but the basic overview of making any Banana Bread is this:

  1. 1. Mix all the dry ingredients in one bowl; the mash banana, milk, and cinnamon in another bowl; adding the rest but not the chocolate chip in another bowl but adding the eggs one at a time.
  2. 2. Stir the banana mixture into the batter mixture; then add the flour mixture and then add the chocolate chips.
  3. 3.Pour the batter in a loaf pan and bake it until the toothpick comes out clean.

The more descriptive direction

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 325 degree Fahrenheit (165 degree Celsius) Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan or for better result glass.
  2. 2. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt in a bowl. stir bananas, milk, and cinnamon in another bowl. Beat the sugar and butter together till it's light and fluffy, then add egg one at a time and keep on beat while stirring the banana mixture into the butter mixture and add the flour until it's blended completely and finally add the chocolate chip.
  3. 3. Pour the batter into the pan. bake it for about 70 minutes, or insert a toothpick and if it comes out clean then it's cooked. Let it cool for about 10 minutes before cutting it up.
