Hash Brown and Bacon Omelet Cup

Looking for a way to reach your protein intake without having to cook much. This Omelet cup have 15 grams of protein per serving and it is super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 3 cups frozen shredded hash brown
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 sups shredded Mexican-style four-cheese blend
  • 6 lightly beaten large eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 6 sliced of bacon, crisp cooked and chopped up
  • freshly chopped chives

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degree Fahrenheit, 220 degree Celsius. With the cooking spray, grease 12 (2 1/2 inch) muffin cups.
  2. Remove the moisture from the hash brown by wrapping and patting with a clean towel. Mix together the hash brown with the melted butter, salt in a large bowl. Pour 1/4 cup of the mixture in the muffin cups.
  3. Put it in the oven and let it cook for 18-20 minutes or until lightly brown.
  4. While it's cooking, stir together the cheese, eggs, and bell pepper in a bowl.
  5. Once the hash brown is ready, add bacon and then the egg mixture. Lower the heat to 400 degrees Fahrenheit. Cook it for 13 to 15 minutes or until the knife comes out clean when being inserted and garnish with Chives.
